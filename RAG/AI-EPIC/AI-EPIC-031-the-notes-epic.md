# AI-EPIC-031-the-notes-epic

## Problem Statement

The v0.25.0 feel pass's largest finding, in the owner's words: "the
notes are not usable at all." The note experience fell through the
seam between "kit drew it" and "wave shipped it" — only the
bound-posture reading flight (AI-IMP-296) ever got a carrier
ticket. Everything since has been building this epic's charter:
RFC rev 0.73 ratified the posture verbs (§8.5: FLY TO READ →
PIN TO THE WORLD → TAPE TO THE GLASS), THE PROPORTION LAW
(§8.8.5: measure-sovereign width, optical centering,
minimum-measure floor, the floating-window law), and the Note
Paper kit closed out in package 1.6 with the edges and zoom-ruler
specimens. The debt is now pure implementation: the drawn,
ratified note experience versus the shipped one.

## Proposed Solution / Feature Scope

Bring every note posture into compliance with the ratified record,
and make the editor worth living in. The known deltas, pre-census:

1. **The bound page obeys the proportion law** — width derives
   from the measure (~45–65ch at its own type size), NEVER from
   the image's aspect; equal-height bind with torn edge on the
   separated side; the tall-portrait needle case is impossible by
   construction (kit specimen is the proof drawing).
2. **The floating postures obey the floating-window law** — the
   sticky/panel header bar fills the surface's width; the surface
   hugs its content (no reserved emptiness); minimum honest size
   240×140, below which it stows to its opener.
3. **The zoom ruler enforced** — every posture renders its ruled
   rung at 100/50/25/8% (live text → rings-only/text-as-texture →
   bound-edge stroke → dot census; sticky never scales), with the
   ruler specimen as the acceptance drawing.
4. **The non-bound presentation** (old AI-IMP-194's scope, ruled
   in the 1.5 wireframes): the standard panel opened from the note
   charm; same panel for non-canvas notes via the charm-bar door.
5. **The big editor earns its role** — the double-click tear to
   centered editor over the dimmed board, polished; its exact role
   (essay-length, keyboard-first) is the one DESIGN-QUEUE residual
   this epic must force to a ruling rather than inherit.
6. **Zoom residuals ruled and landed** — tiny-image cap at reading
   zoom; the OS-zoom text-axis Parking Lot item folds in or dies
   here (queue-annotated 2026-07-16).
7. **Editor quality** — the actual writing surface (the thing the
   first tester called unusable): caret/selection feel, wiki-link
   flow, the escalation sequence's beats.

## Path(s) Forward

Pre-cut census first (this epic's round-0): read the shipped note
surfaces against the Note Paper kit + grammar and §8.5/§8.8.5,
enumerate the per-posture deltas with citations, and cut the
ticket range from the census — not from memory. Tickets are
AI-IMP-307+ (reserved from here; the stragglers wave holds 306).
Expect one wave of 4–6 tickets after the census; the big-editor
role ruling goes to the owner BEFORE its ticket cuts. The dock
promise-ledger pattern extends: a note-paper promise family
drafts alongside the wave (the pins the feel pass wished it had).

## Success Metrics

The first tester can write in a note without wanting to leave;
every posture matches its kit drawing at every zoom rung; the
needle case cannot be reproduced at any image aspect; no note
surface violates the floating-window law; suite floors hold and
the wave's evidence bundle carries per-posture captures keyed to
the new promise family.
