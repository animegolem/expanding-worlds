1gCursor zones — no drawn handles, the cursor IS the affordance
img
inside: ✥ move
edge ±4px: ⇔ resize
corner: ⤡ resize
just OUTSIDE corner: ↻ rotate
empty canvas: grab/pan
Zones, not handles:
· inside ⇒ move cursor
· within 4px of edge ⇒ directional resize (⇕ ⇔)
· corner ⇒ diagonal resize (⤡ ⤢)
· 4–14px OUTSIDE a corner ⇒ rotate cursor ↻
· ⌥ inside ⇒ duplicate-drag
· locked node ⇒ 🚫 cursor, no state to draw

Selected = thin blue outline only. Zero knobs cluttering the art.
Matches your instinct: kill the stick-out rotate handle; hot zones + cursor glyphs communicate everything. Snappy because nothing has to render.
