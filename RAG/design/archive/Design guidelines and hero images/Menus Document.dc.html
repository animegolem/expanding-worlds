1The context-menu grammar — per-kind verb inventories (Design-letter-3 #1 · EPIC-016 core · §8.4/§6.5/§6.7)

The grammar, before the inventories: verbs only, never nouns where avoidable, never "file". Ordering is frequency-first inside meaning groups; groups divide with rules; destructive verbs sit LAST behind a divider, alone. Shortcuts print in mono where they exist. Submenus only for families (Appearance, Tags, Align, Sort). Every verb is one undoable command; multi-placement impacts confirm with the count as a fact. The PureRef reference is adopted selectively — its verb breadth, not its nesting depth.
