1Interaction physics — every mouse-down on the canvas, and the beat (if any) that sells the desk

The promise: the canvas feels like objects with physics; the UI is a terminal. Six physical verbs cover everything — lift · settle · nudge · press · tear · glide — each one-shot, each on world content only, each answering a specific mouse-down. Chrome (marquee, selection outline, panels, tools mid-use) stays beat-free on purpose: live manipulation IS its own feedback, and a beat would be noise.
